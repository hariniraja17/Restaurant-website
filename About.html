About Us

Welcome to INDIAN RESTAURANT, is a renowned non-vegetarian restaurant that has become famous for its exceptional biriyani. with over 20 outlets across Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Andhra Pradesh & Karnataka. Our brand tagline. "World's Best Biriyani" speaks to our Commitment to delivering the most delicious and authemetic biriyani experience.Since opening our first outlet in 2000, we have grown from a humble single shop to a beloved brand trusted by biriyani lovers around the world.

Features

  1. 1. Authentic Indian Cuisine: Offering a wide variety of traditional Indian dishes, prepared with authentic spices, flavors, and cooking techniques, showcasing the diversity of Indian culinary traditions.

  2. 2. Menu Diversity: Providing a diverse menu that caters to different dietary preferences, including vegetarian, vegan, and non-vegetarian options, as well as various regional specialties.

  3. 3. Rich Flavors and Spices: Emphasizing the use of aromatic spices such as cumin, coriander, turmeric, cardamom, and garam masala, which are key to creating the distinctive and flavorful taste of Indian cuisine.


  4. 4. Warm Hospitality: Providing a welcoming and hospitable atmosphere where guests feel valued and appreciated, with attentive staff who are knowledgeable about the menu and can offer recommendations.


  5. 5. Cultural Ambiance: Incorporating elements of Indian culture and aesthetics into the restaurant's decor, music, and ambiance, creating an immersive dining experience for guests.


  6. 6. Tandoori Cooking: Offering tandoori dishes cooked in a traditional clay oven (tandoor), including tandoori chicken, naan bread, and kebabs, which are known for their smoky flavor and tender texture.


  7. 7. Fresh Ingredients: Using high-quality, fresh ingredients sourced locally whenever possible, to ensure the authenticity and freshness of the dishes.


  8. 8. Customization Options: Providing options for customizing spice levels and dish preferences to accommodate individual tastes and dietary restrictions.


  9. 9. Specialized Beverages: Offering a selection of Indian beverages such as lassi (yogurt-based drink), chai (spiced tea), and various alcoholic beverages including Indian beer and cocktails to complement the meal.


  10. 10. Dessert Selection: Featuring traditional Indian desserts such as gulab jamun, rasgulla, and kulfi, as well as fusion desserts that incorporate Indian flavors into Western-style desserts.


Overall, an Indian restaurant aims to provide a memorable dining experience by offering delicious and authentic Indian cuisine in a welcoming atmosphere that celebrates the rich culinary heritage of India.
